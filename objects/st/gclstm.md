# Graph Convolutional LSTM (GCLSTM)

**Dataset**: Chickenpox

GCLSTM integrates graph convolutional layers into LSTMs to model spatial-temporal dependencies in graph-structured time series data.

## Results on Chickenpox Dataset:

- **MSE**: 1.1536
- **MAE**: 0.6915
- **R-squared**: -0.0925
