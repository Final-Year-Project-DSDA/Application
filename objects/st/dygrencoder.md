# Dynamic Graph Encoder (DyGrEncoder)

**Dataset**: Chickenpox

DyGrEncoder is designed to dynamically encode evolving graphs for forecasting tasks by capturing both spatial and temporal structures.

## Results on Chickenpox Dataset:

- **MSE**: 0.9655
- **MAE**: 0.6355
- **MAPE**: 1060.5135%
- **RMSE**: 0.9826
- **R-squared**: 0.0411
